Omelette Recipe

Description

Omelette is one of the most simple dishes one can make at their kitchen with the least amount of ingredients. It is also one of the tastiest dishes out there with its own unique taste that is unlike any other. In this page, we are going to see how to cook this dish.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. First heat a pan.
  2. Then pour some oil unto it and let it get hot.
  3. While the oil's heating, mix the egg and salt in some cup and stir it well.
  4. Then pour the egg onto the pan once the oil's heated.
  5. Flip the omlette to let it cook evenly
  6. Done!
