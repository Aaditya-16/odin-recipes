Tea Recipe

Description

Tea is a very popular dish in all over the world. While there are many sources of origins one can hear about, most would argue that it originated from China and was originally called chai. Atleast that's what I have heard if I recall correctly.

In this webpage you are going to find the detailed instructions to make your very own tea at home. The steps are very simple and the ingredients are also very easy to fine. Chances are you already have the ingredients at home! So without further ado, let us dive right it.

Ingredients

  • Tea leaves
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Milk

Steps

  1. First boil some waters.
  2. Then add tea leaves.
  3. Then add sugar
  4. Let it shimmer for some time
  5. Then add milk
  6. Now let it shimmer for a long time to let the flavors spread
  7. Done!
