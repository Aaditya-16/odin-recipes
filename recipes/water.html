Hot Water Recipe

Description

Water is one of the most important necessities for life. One cannot imagine a life without it, be it for humans or animals. Hot water is a luxury that we relish in the cold winter days. It can truly be relaxing.

In this page, we are going to see how to boil water and make it hot.

Ingredients

  • Water

Steps

  1. First put the required amount of water in a pan.
  2. Then put the pan in the stove after lighting it.
  3. Once the water starts bubbling, it means its boiled.
  4. Done!
